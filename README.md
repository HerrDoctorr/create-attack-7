The seventh season of the popular Create Attack series is here! Create Attack 7 – The Factory Must Grow takes the experience to a whole new level: bigger, more ambitious, and developed by HerrDoctor together with a dedicated team.

![CA7-IMG_Divider](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/c76f3bb1d7157b0168af69eae4282e56113943db.png) 

![CA7-IMG_002](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/evRak9Zo/images/895d77262403d2cc705ce1e1e98f1858bfcd253d.png) 

![CA7-IMG_Divider](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/c76f3bb1d7157b0168af69eae4282e56113943db.png)





- **Technology & Automation**
At its core, this modpack revolves around the Create mod, expanded by a massive collection of addons such as Create: Steam ’n’ Rails, Big Cannons, Diesel Generators, Pattern Schematics, Connected, and of course The Factory Must Grow.
Design intricate contraptions, automate your production, and scale up to massive industrial complexes.

- **Exploration & Adventure**
Your world will feel alive and challenging with mods like Terralith, Nullscape, Incendium, and the complete set of YUNG’s Better Structures (Dungeons, Strongholds, Temples, Fortresses, Mineshafts, Monuments, Witch Huts, and more).
Every exploration run promises new landscapes, improved structures, and breathtaking building opportunities.

- **Organization & Quality of Life**
Refined Storage, Storage Drawers, and Sophisticated Backpacks keep your resources perfectly managed.

- Xaero’s Minimap & Worldmap plus Nature’s Compass make navigation simple and efficient.

- Multiplayer & Community Features

- Simple Voice Chat (proximity-based) for immersive communication.

- Open Parties & Claims for collaboration and land protection.

- FTB Quests & Teams for structured progression and cooperative play.

- **Atmosphere & Immersion**
  From AmbientSounds and Rechiseled to Immersive Aircraft, Farm & Charm, Vinery, and Bakery,
  the world feels more detailed and vibrant than ever.
  A customized FancyMenu ties the whole experience together.

- **Performance & Stability**
  Packed with optimizations like FerriteCore, ModernFix, Embeddium, Sodium, and Dynamic FPS,
  this modpack ensures smooth gameplay even with massive factories and large-scale builds.
